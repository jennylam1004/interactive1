Facebook's Evolution

The growth that facebook went through, shows how much that they have progressed. All of their new features shows how much the current demographic uses and expects social media to become. Where you can like and comment on your friends page, message them privately, having group chats, to now Where you can send money through the chatting systems, send stickers or even play games via the messenger app. Facebook has went through many developments to add more to their platform in order to appease to the growing consumer.
